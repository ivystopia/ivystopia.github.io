# LEGO Cheat Sheet

## Company & Name

- **Founded:** 1932, Ole Kirk Christiansen, Billund, Denmark
- **Name origin:** “LEGO” = _leg godt_ = “play well” (Danish)
- **Ownership:** Kristiansen family (private)

## Bricks

- **First plastic bricks:** 1949 (“Automatic Binding Bricks”)
- **Modern brick patent:** 1958-01-28
- **Compatibility:** Since 1958
- **2×4 brick size:** 31.8 mm × 15.8 mm × 9.6 mm
- **Material:** ABS (since 1963)
- **Tolerance:** ±0.002–0.004 mm
- **2×4 combinations (6 bricks):** 915,103,765

## Minifigures

- **Introduced:** 1978 (Set #600 – police)
- **Height:** ~4 cm
- **Produced:** >4 billion
- **First female:** 1978 (nurse)
- **Rare figs:** Mr. Gold (5,000 made), Chrome C-3PO (10,000 made)

## Themes & Product Lines

- **Duplo:** 1969
- **Technic:** 1977
- **Town (→City):** 1978
- **First licensed theme:** Star Wars (1999)
- **Other licensed:** Harry Potter (2001), Batman (2006)
- **Architecture:** 2008
- **LEGO Ideas:** 2008

## Largest Sets (by piece count)

- **#31203 World Map:** 11,695 pcs
- **#10307 Eiffel Tower:** 10,001 pcs (tallest)
- **#10294 Titanic:** 9,090 pcs
- **#10276 Colosseum:** 9,036 pcs
- **#75192 UCS Millennium Falcon:** 7,541 pcs

## Records

- **Tallest tower:** 35.05 m (Milan, 2015)
- **Largest model:** Life-size X-Wing (~5.3 million bricks, 2013)

## Production Stats

- **Bricks/year:** ~36 billion
- **Bricks/minute:** ~68,000
- **Bricks/second:** ~1,140
- **Tires/year:** ~300 million (largest by unit count)
- **Bricks per capita:** ~80
- **Unique elements (molds):** >3,700
- **Colors:** 60+

## Digital & Media

- **First game:** LEGO Island (1997)
- **Digital Designer launched:** 2004
- **LEGO Movie released:** 2014

## Parks & Retail

- **First Legoland park:** Billund, 1968
- **Parks licensed to:** Merlin Entertainments
- **LEGO Stores:** Company-owned worldwide

## Sustainability

- **Bio-PE parts (sugarcane):** Introduced 2018
- **Recycled elements:** In progress
- **Sustainability goal:** All products sustainable by ~2030

## Business & Operations

- **Top toy company by revenue:** Since 2015
- **2024 revenue:** DKK 74.3B (~$10.8B)
- **Employees:** ~24,500
- **Factories in:** DK, HU, MX, CN, VN, USA (planned)
